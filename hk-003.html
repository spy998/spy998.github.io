TRANSCRIPT

Royal Waterways
These sewers were brimming with refuse as I thought.
Though, what I did not expect to find among the crawling creatures and the waist of the city above was a mighty and nobel warrior.
I have experieced no battle like that, the Mantis Lords were testing me, the responsibility to their kingdom giving our battle weight.
There was no such weight trading blows with this Dung Defender. Like the sing songy bellows I could hear down the tunnel before I even saw him he battled with a springy lightness that was surprising with the stoutness of his form.
He fought with purpose, no one who is this good doesn't, but he also clearly enjoyed himself. Like a dancer on the stage it was as if he was performing for his people just as much as barring me passage.
I eventually emerged victorious, but unlike most others I did not slay him. I sensed no ill intent from him, he was merely fulfilling his duty and taking joy in that fulfillment.
I hope to see him again perhaps. Who knows where this journey will take me after all.
