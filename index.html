Documenting My Gaming Journey



About This Blog


I want to engage with the games I play in a different way.

I'm an adult, with a job, and many social obligations. So time to sit down and play a game is rare.

When I set aside two or three hours to play something (usually with my wife joining me), I want to truly engage with that game. What its trying to tell me, the story and vibes.

So, when I finish any of these sessions, I get out a journal, and I write.

Not just to summarize what happened (there's plenty of youtube videos that people listen to more than watch out there that do that). I want to immerse myself in the story.

So, I write from the perspective of the game character, attempt to meld my thoughts and feelings with what I believe theirs to be. Engage with the plot and the themes put forth.

That's where this blog comes in, I want to catalog and document these writings, mostly for myself, but also I hope that this can inspire anyone who may come across this place to engage with games in a deeper way.

There will not be a common theme here, the games I play and write about will be a wide variety.

I will tag what the game is in each post and of course make clear on my posts and here that these journal entries will contain spoilers to the game in question.

Plese note: These entries will be hand written, I will provide pictures of the pages but also a transcript of the words as I am all too aware my handwriting is not the most legible.
