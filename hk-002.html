This area wasn't that large but the enemey flavor and the boss really stuck with me so this entry is meatier.

I of course am not fully aware of the lore, this is a blind playthrough and I haven't even consumed Youtube videos about Hollow Knight.

So these swings I'm making on the lore may very well be proven way off base later down the line, though, I can just chalk that up to Little Ghost not knowing at first either.

TRANSCRIPT

Soul Sanctum
I've smelled the rot of death, this is differnt.
There is a stark difference between the wilting of a plant, and the stench of spoiled fruit.
This place reeks of that stench. Things long past their time ruining everything on the shelves around them.
What used to hold tomes and books for study is only home to scattered papers now; and like the scattered paper are the creatures that float and crawl through these chambers
I can tell that at one time they were whole, but like these sheets I cannot tell what that whole even looked like.
All of this over soul. An insatiable greed for it. Or, more accurately, the power it grants.
Though, this power did not make them strong or formidable, it made them caustic and annoying.
Especially the master near the top. His form was bloated with soul, his very being volatile and fighting the space around him just as much as it was fighting me.
Even after I had slayed him the soul that remained insisted on continuing to struggle against all around it. It eventually became absorbed into me, like other essences before it.
That much soul left things here deformed, countless failed attempts to absorb and channel it.
My form has no such issues. Soul flys into me as if eager to be absorbed. The abilities soul grants me are as easy to do as jumping or swinging my nail.
After what I've seen here maybe that should concern me. For some reason it does not.
